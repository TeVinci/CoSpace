Why is it?

  • Collaboration is the key to human development
  • If people do what they like to do, their productivity often exceeds expectations
  • Giving every human the possibility to show what he/she can do, helps people to find their real passion (inducing happiness)
  • The ongoing globalization requires a new approach to collaboration
    • The old hierarchical way of organizing projects is outdated
    • Worldwide collaboration is only possible if people see how dependent they are on each other
      • There is no way anyone of us could build any of the modern high-tech machines etc. On his/her own.
      • Every involved individuum in a project is thus necessary to make the project work
        • Therefore no one should have the power to decide over someone else
